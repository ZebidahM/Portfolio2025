About Me

I design insightful, functional solutions with data.

I'm a data scientist in the making, with a passion for turning raw information into real-world impact. I started out as a developer working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building clean, functional digital experiences. Now, I’m diving deep into data, driven by a curiosity to uncover insights and solve meaningful problems. Currently training in data science, I’m learning to work with Python, SQL, and machine learning tools to build models that inform decisions and spark innovation. I’m especially interested in applying data to areas like public health, education, and urban development, where tech can make life better. Problem-solving is at the heart of what I do. I love breaking down complex challenges and finding patterns others might miss. I believe in learning by doing, so every project is a chance to grow, create value, and push boundaries. My goal is to become a data scientist who not only crunches numbers but also tells stories with data,stories that drive action, create opportunity, and move communities forward. Let’s build something that matters.
